## Rules Reference

  
An asteroid field is an anomaly that affects movement.

 
 1. A ship cannot move through or into an asteroid field.
 
 
## Notes

 
 1. A player with the <i>Antimass Deflectors</i> technology may move their ships through asteroid fields.
 2. A future rule change will place the ship movement restrictions of anomalies upon all units.
