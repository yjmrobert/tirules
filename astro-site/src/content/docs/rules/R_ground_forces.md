## Rules Reference

  
A ground force is a type of unit. All infantry and mech units in the game are ground forces. Some races have unique infantry units.

 
1. Ground forces are always on planets, in a space area with ships that have capacity values, or being transported by those ships.
2. Ground forces being transported by a ship are placed in a systemâ€™s space area along with the ship that is transporting them.
3. There is no limit to the number of ground forces a player can have on a planet.
