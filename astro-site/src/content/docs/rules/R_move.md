## Rules Reference

  
Move is an attribute of some units that is presented on faction sheets and unit upgrade technology cards.

 
 1. A unit’s move value indicates the distance from its current system that it can move during the <b>Movement</b> step of a tactical action.
 
 
## Notes

 
 1. When an effect moves a unit to an adjacent system, modifying that unit’s move value will have no effect.
